Select a course to play!

  • First you have to choose a course and then a module. Then you will come in to a ready state and wait for the game start. The game will start within 15 seconds when there are at least 2 players in the game. Otherwise you have to wait until another one joins the game or you can exit to choose another course or module.
  • All players in the same game will compete against each other. You will get 15 seconds for each question to answer. Your score will be shown at the end of the game.
